Research Direction

My research primarily revolves around numerical simulations in fluid dynamics and topology optimization. I have expertise in:

  • SIMP Method
  • Phase-field Method
  • Homogenization Techniques

These methods are applied to solve complex fluid dynamics problems and optimize structural designs for improved performance.
