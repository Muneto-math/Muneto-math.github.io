About Me

Welcome to my personal website! My name is Liu Zongyuan. I completed my undergraduate degree in Physics at East China Normal University. During my undergraduate studies, I worked on two main projects:

  • Theoretical Physics: Investigating quantum thermalization of a single atom in a strong magnetic field.
  • Experimental Physics: Exploring laser-induced photonic crystals and their applications in facial recognition.

After finishing my undergraduate coursework, I delved into more advanced fields through self-study, which highlighted my need for a stronger mathematical foundation. This realization led me to pursue a degree in Mathematics at East China Normal University. My curiosity about understanding the world continues to drive my research and exploration.

Research

I am currently focused on numerical simulations in fluid dynamics and topology optimization. My expertise includes methods such as SIMP, phase-field, and homogenization techniques. I regularly update this section with my latest research and publications.
